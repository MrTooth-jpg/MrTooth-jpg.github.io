Goal 2: Zero Hunger

End hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture by 2030.

Why is Zero Hunger Important?

Hunger and malnutrition affect millions of people worldwide. Ensuring food security and proper nutrition is essential for human health, economic development, and social stability.

Key Facts

  • Over 800 million people worldwide suffer from hunger.
  • Malnutrition is a leading cause of death in children under five.
  • Sustainable agriculture practices can help ensure long-term food security.

How Can We Achieve This Goal?

  • Support local farmers and sustainable agriculture initiatives.
  • Reduce food waste by adopting responsible consumption habits.
  • Encourage policies that ensure food access for all, especially vulnerable populations.
