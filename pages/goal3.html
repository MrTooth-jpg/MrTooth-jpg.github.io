Goal 3: Good Health and Well-being

Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages by 2030.

Why is Good Health and Well-being Important?

Good health and well-being are essential for a fulfilling life. SDG 3 focuses on providing access to quality healthcare, reducing mortality rates, and promoting mental and physical well-being for everyone, regardless of age or background.

Key Facts

  • 1 in 10 people worldwide lack access to basic healthcare services.
  • Maternal mortality has decreased by 38% globally since 2000.
  • More than 264 million people suffer from depression globally.

How Can We Achieve This Goal?

  • Ensure universal health coverage (UHC) for all individuals and communities.
  • Strengthen healthcare systems to provide essential services for all.
  • Promote mental health awareness and integrate it into healthcare services.
  • Improve access to essential medicines and vaccines.
  • Combat preventable diseases through vaccination and education programs.
