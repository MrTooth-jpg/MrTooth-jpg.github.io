Goal 1: No Poverty

Poverty is more than the lack of income and resources. It is a complex issue that includes hunger, malnutrition, limited access to education and healthcare, social discrimination, and lack of participation in decision-making.

As of today, over 700 million people still live in extreme poverty, surviving on less than $1.90 a day.

By 2030, the goal is to eradicate extreme poverty for all people everywhere, implement social protection systems, and ensure equal access to economic resources.

Key Facts

  • 10% of the world's population lives in extreme poverty.
  • Children are disproportionately affected, with millions suffering from malnutrition and lack of education.
  • Women are more likely to live in poverty due to wage gaps and social discrimination.
  • Climate change and conflicts are major contributors to poverty worldwide.

How Can We Help?

  • Support fair trade and ethical businesses.
  • Donate to organizations fighting poverty.
  • Advocate for policies that support economic equality.
  • Encourage education and vocational training programs.
