Goal 5: Gender Equality

Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls by 2030.

Why is Gender Equality Important?

Gender equality is essential for a peaceful, prosperous, and sustainable world. Achieving gender equality benefits all people by promoting equal opportunities, access to resources, and decision-making power. It is not only a fundamental human right but also crucial for building stronger economies and societies.

Key Facts

  • One in three women worldwide have experienced physical or sexual violence.
  • Women earn, on average, 20% less than men for similar work.
  • Only 25% of parliamentary seats are held by women globally.

How Can We Achieve This Goal?

  • Ensure equal access to education, healthcare, and employment opportunities for women and girls.
  • End all forms of violence and discrimination against women and girls.
  • Promote equal participation of women in leadership roles and decision-making processes.
  • Enhance policies and programs that support gender equality in all sectors, including political, economic, and social domains.
  • Challenge harmful gender stereotypes and promote positive gender norms through education and awareness programs.
