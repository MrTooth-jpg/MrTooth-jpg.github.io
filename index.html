What is the 2030 Agenda?

The 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development is an action plan adopted by the UN in 2015 with the goal of improving people's lives and protecting the planet. It contains 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) aimed at eradicating poverty, protecting the environment, and ensuring prosperity for all.
